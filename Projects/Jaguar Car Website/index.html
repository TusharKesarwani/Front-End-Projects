DESCRIPTION TO OUR NEW FAMILIES

Jaguar is the luxury vehicle brand of Jaguar Land Rover, a British multinational car manufacturer with its headquarters in Whitley, Coventry, England, owned by the Indian company Tata Motors.

Jaguar's business was founded as the Swallow Sidecar Company in 1922,

originally making motorcycle sidecars before developing bodies for passenger cars. Under the ownership of S. S. Cars Limited

the business extended to complete cars made in association with Standard Motor Co, many bearing Jaguar as a model name. The companyâ€™s name was changed from S. S. Cars to Jaguar Cars in 1945.
