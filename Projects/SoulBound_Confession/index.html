Services

SCREENING & ASSESSMENT

Screening and assessment are essential components of any effective intervention or treatment program. Screening is a preliminary process that is used to identify individuals who may be at risk of a particular condition or problem.

COUNSELLING SESSIONS

Counseling sessions are a safe and confidential space for individuals to explore their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors with a trained professional.individuals gain insight into their challenges, and develop strategies for managing difficult situations.

YOGA & PHYSIOTHERAPY

Yoga and physiotherapy are two practices that can greatly benefit a person's physical and mental well-being. Yoga involves a series of postures, breathing exercises, and meditation techniques that can improve flexibility, strength, balance, and relaxation.

VIDEO CONFERENCING SESSIONS

Video conferencing sessions have become increasingly popular in recent years, allowing people to connect face-to-face from anywhere in the world. Video conferencing has become an essential tool for collaboration and communication.

Understanding and Coping with Anxiety: Tips and Techniques

Anxiety is a common experience that can range from mild to severe and interfere with daily life. Understanding the causes and symptoms of anxiety is the first step towards managing it effectively. Some common causes of anxiety include stress, genetics, and traumatic experiences. Symptoms can include excessive worry, restlessness, difficulty concentrating, and physical symptoms such as sweating or a racing heart.

Mental Health Stigma: Breaking Down Barriers to Treatment

Mental health stigma is a significant barrier to treatment, preventing many individuals from seeking help and receiving the care they need. It can be incredibly isolating and can leave individuals feeling ashamed or embarrassed about their mental health concerns. Stigma can take many forms, including discrimination, stereotypes, and negative attitudes towards those with mental health issues.
